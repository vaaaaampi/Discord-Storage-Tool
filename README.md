# Discord Storage Bot
A bot that makes a new use for discord as Unlimited Cloud Storage

## Setup

1. Go to https://discord.com/developers/applications and click on "New Application"

2. Give it any name, and click create
3. On the left side, click bot
4. Enable all privileged gateway intents
5. Give it administrator permissions
6. On the left side, go to OAuth2, check "bot" and then check "Administrator"
7. Copy the link in down and add it to your server
8. Copy your bot's token, then go on "server.js" line 10 and paste your token
9. Go to your server, right click on the channel you want the files to be sent on and Copy the ID
10. Go to "server.js" line 11 and paste your ID
11. Open a terminal, and run "node ./server.js"
12. Go to http://localhost:3000
13. Enjoy !

## DISCLAIMER

This is slow as heck, it's **NOT RECOMMENDED NOR INTENTED** for usage with important or large files.
